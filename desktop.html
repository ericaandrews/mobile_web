WURFL Capability: is_wireless_device
-comes with three possible values: true, false, NULL
-capability will be set to false if it's a laptop even if the laptop has wireless network card.
WURFL Capability: has_cellular_radio
-capability from a group called bearer
-shows if the request is coming from a mobile device with phone capability or not
-capability tested to decide if the "panic button" would be shown to the user or not
WURFL Capability: can_assign_phone_number
-capability(true or false)
-showing its a mobile phone and MAY have a phone number associated to it.
